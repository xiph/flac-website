Although being designed to contain audio, FLAC can compress other kinds of signals very well too, like measurement signals. In some scientific publications/presentations, FLAC is used to contain such signals, such as:

  • South Pole Telescope:
    • The Design and Integrated Performance of SPT-3G
    • Extreme digitisation for ground-based cosmic microwave background experiments
  • Context-Aware Lossless and Lossy Compression of Radio Frequency Signals
  • Compressing raw video tape data with FLAC
